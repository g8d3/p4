## Recommendations

### Best Overall: **OpenMontage** (31.3k ⭐)
Most comprehensive agentic video production system. Only drawback: AGPL license.

### Best Open Model: **Wan2.2** (16.5k ⭐) or **CogVideo** (12.8k ⭐)
Apache-2.0, state-of-the-art quality, can be self-hosted.

### Best Studio: **Open-Generative-AI** (22.1k ⭐)
200+ models in one UI, MIT license, no content filters.

### Best for Short Dramas: **Toonflow-app** (11k ⭐) or **waoowaoo** (13k ⭐)
End-to-end pipeline from script to video.

### Best for Avatars: **LivePortrait** (18.7k ⭐) or **Duix-Avatar** (13.8k ⭐)
Face animation and digital human video generation.

### Best for Translation/Dubbing: **KrillinAI** (10.4k ⭐)
100+ languages, multi-platform support.
