## Audio / Voice models (TTS)

Credit costs not listed in pricing table (may be included in plan).

| Model | Description | Best for |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| **ElevenLabs Eleven v3** | Emotion and delivery control via inline tags. Premium quality. Voice presets available (Tallulah, etc.) | Narration, voiceover, emotional speech |
| **MiniMax Speech 2.8 HD** | High-fidelity single-voice narration | Clean narration |
| **VibeVoice** | Long-form narration for audiobooks and podcasts | Long recordings |
| **Seed Speech** | Multilingual speech across 30+ languages | Multi-language |
| **Seed Audio 1.0** (NEW) | Multi-speaker scenes with speech and ambience | Complex audio scenes |

ElevenLabs Eleven v3 is the best TTS available on the platform. For simple
narration, MiniMax Speech 2.8 HD or VibeVoice may be cheaper alternatives
with 80-90% quality. Credit costs not publicly listed — test small first.
